Definition 1.1.9. Linear Equation.

A linear equation is an equation that can be written in the form
\begin{equation*} a_1x_1+a_2x_2+\cdots+a_nx_n = c \end{equation*}
where the \(x_i\) are variables (the unknowns), the \(a_i\) are coefficients (numbers), and \(c\) is a constant (also a number).
